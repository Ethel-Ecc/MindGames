Mind Games

An algorithm has been used to select a random number [Integer Numbers Only] that is between one[1] and One-Hundred[100].
Your task is to accurately guess the number generated by the algorithm. You have seven[7] attempts to use your"Mind" to guess the right number. The less attempts you use, the higher your rank in the online score board. Each submitted guess comes with a system generated hint on the proximity of your guess to the answer.
Good Luck and have fun!!!
